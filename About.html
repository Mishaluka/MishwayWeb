A personal shopper is also known as a "stylist" or "fashion consultant", and is one who provides image makeover and fashion consultation services to celebrities, fashion models, and personal clients with personal taste. They hand select the finest clothing and accessories for public figures, models, celebrities as well as for their personal clients and help them to enhance their image and personal appearance. They are often assisted by a team of fashion designers.
